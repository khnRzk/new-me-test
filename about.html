Muslim Matrimony Service – Kandy & Central Province, Sri Lanka

Akurana Nikkah Service is a free, trusted Islamic matrimony service based in Akurana, Kandy, Sri Lanka. We help Muslim families in Central Province and across Sri Lanka find righteous, compatible life partners — through a simple, transparent, and fully halal process.

Every profile on our platform is submitted directly by the individual or their family, carefully reviewed by our team, and published only with their consent. Submitting and listing your profile is completely free. A reasonable charge applies when a serious enquirer requests the full contact details of a profile — ensuring genuine interest and protecting the privacy of our members.

How It Works

1
Browse Profiles View verified Muslim Groom and Bride profiles — filter by location, marital status, or search by education and occupation. No login needed.
2
Note the Ref No When you find a suitable profile, copy or note the Ref No shown on the card (e.g. AKNS-0270).
3
Reach Out via WhatsApp Tap the WhatsApp button — your message is pre-filled with the Ref No. Our team will facilitate a proper, halal introduction between families.
4
Submit Your Own Profile Tap "Submit Your Profile" and send your details in any format via WhatsApp. We will review, format, and publish your profile within 24 hours — free of charge, InshāAllah.
