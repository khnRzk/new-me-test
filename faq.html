Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about Akurana Nikkah Service.
1
What is Akurana Nikkah Service?
Akurana Nikkah Service is a free Islamic matrimony service based in Akurana, Kandy, Sri Lanka. We connect verified Muslim Grooms and Brides in Central Province and across Sri Lanka through a halal, family-friendly process — completely free of charge, Alhamdulillah.
2
Is this service free to use?
Browsing profiles and submitting your own profile is completely free. A reasonable charge applies when a serious enquirer requests the full contact details of a profile. This helps ensure genuine interest and protects the privacy of our members.
3
Which areas do you cover?
We primarily serve Central Province — including Kandy, Akurana, Gampola, Matale, Peradeniya, Katugastota, Kundasale, and Nuwara Eliya. We also welcome and list profiles from other provinces across Sri Lanka.
4
How do I submit my profile?
Tap the "Submit Your Profile" button on the home page and send your details via WhatsApp in any format you prefer — a simple list, a paragraph, or even voice notes. Profile submission and listing is free. Our team will review, format, and publish your profile within 24 hours, InshāAllah.
5
How do I enquire about a profile I like?
Open the profile card on our website and tap the green WhatsApp button. Your message will be automatically pre-filled with the profile's Ref No. Our team will then facilitate a proper, halal introduction between the families.
6
Are the profiles verified?
Yes. Every profile is submitted directly to our team by the individual or their family, reviewed carefully for completeness and authenticity, and published only with full consent. We do not list profiles without verification.
7
How long does it take to publish my profile?
Profiles are typically reviewed and published within 24 hours of submission, InshāAllah. Once published, your profile will appear at the top of the list with a "NEW" badge for the first 24 hours.
8
Can I search profiles by location or education?
Yes. On our website you can filter profiles by province (Central Province or other), marital status (unmarried or previously married), and type (Groom or Bride). You can also use the search bar to find profiles by location, education, occupation, or Ref No.
9
Is my personal information kept private?
Yes. Full personal contact details are never displayed publicly on the website. Interested parties reach out to our team via WhatsApp, and we facilitate introductions in a discreet and respectful manner. All submissions and enquiries are kept strictly confidential.
10
Can I remove or update my profile?
Yes. Simply contact us via WhatsApp with your Ref No and let us know what changes you need. We can update details or remove your profile entirely at any time — no questions asked.
